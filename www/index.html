⚔️ How to Play
OrbMaster is a code-breaking RPG. Each boss guards a secret sequence of colored orbs. Your job is to crack the code before you run out of turns.
The Core Loop
1. Choose your boss from the Campaign screen.
2. Set your loadout — pick your code depth and equip up to 4 ability orbs.
3. Submit a guess — place colored orbs in your MindBreaker and tap Submit.
4. Read the feedback pegs — they reveal how close your guess was.
5. Use orb abilities strategically to gain hints, extra turns, or scramble the boss.
6. Crack the code within the turn limit to win orb fragments.
7. Spend fragments in the Orb Forge to unlock more powerful orbs.
Win & Loss Conditions
Win: Submit a guess where every orb is the correct color in the correct position.
Lose: Exhaust all your turns without cracking the code.
Feedback Pegs
Red Peg — correct color, correct position
White Peg — correct color, wrong position
No Peg — this color is not in the code at all
Pegs don't tell you which orb slot they belong to — only the total count of reds and whites.
Turn Limit
Each match starts with 10 turns. Certain orb abilities can add extra turns. Boss specials can reduce them.
The faster you crack the code, the more orb fragments you earn as a reward.
MindBreaker Altar
The MindBreaker Altar is your guess console — a magnificent stone altar with orb slots carved into its face. You place your colored orbs into its slots to submit each guess.
Code Depth (Slots)
The number of orb slots determines how many orbs are in the boss's secret code — and in your guess. More slots means a harder puzzle with bigger rewards.
4 Slots — Standard depth. Available from the start.
5 Slots — Advanced depth. Requires the Rank 5 upgrade from the Orb Forge.
6 Slots — Maximum depth. Requires the Rank 6 upgrade from the Orb Forge.
You choose your code depth before each match on the Loadout screen. Going deeper increases your fragment reward multiplier (×1.3 for 5, ×1.7 for 6).
MindBreaker Rank
Rank 1 — Starting rank.
Rank 2 — Earned after defeating Boss 2.
Rank 3 — Earned after defeating Boss 3.
Rank 4 — Four Orbs — Earned after defeating Boss 4. Stays here until upgraded.
Rank 5 — Five Orbs — Purchased in the Orb Forge after defeating Boss 5.
Rank 6 — Six Orbs — Purchased in the Orb Forge after defeating Boss 7.
Forge Upgrade Costs
Rank 5: 150 Orange + 150 Blue + 100 Green + 100 Red fragments
Rank 6: 200 Purple + 150 White + 150 Black + 100 Teal fragments

👹 Boss Profiles
Each boss guards a secret orb code. Defeat them in order to progress the campaign and unlock new colors, ranks, and abilities.
Fragment System
Orb Fragments are the currency of OrbMaster. You earn them by winning matches and spend them in the Orb Forge to craft powerful ability orbs.
Earning Fragments
Each match rewards 4 batches of fragments when you win. The type and amount depend on how quickly you solved the code and the boss's loot table.
Base reward per orb: 50 fragments
Turn penalty: −5 fragments per orb for every turn used after Turn 1
Formula: (50 − ((turns_used − 2) × 5)) × code_size × multiplier
Solving in Turn 1 or 2 gives the maximum reward. Using orb abilities that add turns does not reduce your reward.
Reward Multipliers
5-orb code: ×1.3 bonus to all fragments earned
6-orb code: ×1.7 bonus to all fragments earned
Pink Orb equipped: ×1.75 total multiplier
Teal Orb equipped: ×2.25 total multiplier (does not stack with Pink)
Boss 7+: +20% bonus fragments (late-game economy boost)
Loot Rarity
Each fragment batch is rolled against the boss's loot table to determine its rarity tier — Common, Uncommon, or Rare. Higher rarity means rarer orb types appear in the reward.
Silver Orb (+15% Uncommon) and Gold Orb (+20% Rare) passively shift the loot table in your favor when equipped.
Forging Orbs
Visit the Orb Forge to spend fragments and craft ability orbs. Each orb type has its own fragment cost and requires you to have unlocked that color through campaign progression.
🗺️ Progression & Unlocks
OrbMaster has a full campaign of 10 bosses. Defeating each one unlocks the next and gradually expands your pool of available orb colors and MindBreaker capabilities.
Color Unlock Schedule
Start: Red, Blue, Yellow, Green, Orange
After Boss 3: Silver unlocked
After Boss 4: Gold unlocked
After Boss 5: Purple unlocked
After Boss 6: Black unlocked
After Boss 7: White unlocked
After Boss 8: Pink unlocked
After Boss 9: Teal unlocked
Boss 10 (Mad Martin) is the only boss whose 6-color code can draw from all 12 orb colors — making him the ultimate challenge.
MindBreaker Rank Progression
Rank 1: Starting rank — 4 orb slots
Rank 2: Defeat Boss 2 — 4 orb slots
Rank 3: Defeat Boss 3 — 4 orb slots
Rank 4: Defeat Boss 4 — 4 orb slots (stays here until purchased)
Rank 5: Purchase at Orb Forge after Boss 5 — unlocks 5 orb slots
Rank 6: Purchase at Orb Forge after Boss 7 — unlocks 6 orb slots
Boss Fragment Requirements
Bosses 6–10 require you to have collected specific fragments before you can challenge them. Check the Campaign screen for exact requirements per boss.
Build Archetypes
🧠 Solver Build: Black + Red + Green — maximize information gain per turn.
💰 Economy Build: Pink + Gold + Silver — maximize fragment rewards per match.
🛡️ Control Build: Yellow + Blue + Purple — manipulate the board and buy extra turns.
