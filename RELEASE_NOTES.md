# OrbMaster — Release Notes

## v1.10 (June 2026)

### Play Store "What's new" (short)

> Ability orbs can now be UPGRADED! Forge an upgrade in the Orb Forge to power up each orb — Undertow gains a 2nd charge, Hindsight also reveals a code position, Burn reveals two, and more. Every one of the 12 orbs has an upgrade. Upgrades power your Campaign and Gauntlet runs (the Daily Challenge and Multiplayer stay on base abilities to keep them fair). The Forge now shows each orb's ability name too.

### What's new in 1.10

- **🔺 Ability upgrades** — once you've forged an orb, an upgrade appears in the Orb Forge for its own fragment cost. Upgrades take effect in **Campaign and Gauntlet** (the Daily Challenge and Multiplayer use base abilities, since both are competitive):
  - Orange/Purple/White (extra guesses): one more than before (+2 / +3 / +4).
  - Blue **Undertow**: gains a 2nd charge — use it twice per match.
  - Green **Hindsight**: also reveals 1 random orb that IS in the code.
  - Red **Burn**: reveals 2 positions instead of 1.
  - Yellow **Nullify**: also reveals 1 orb NOT in the code.
  - Pink **Insight**: reveals 3 positions instead of 2.
  - Teal **Purge**: removes 4 wrong colors instead of 3.
  - Black **Pierce**: gains a 2nd charge — choose two positions.
  - Silver/Gold (loot boosts): bigger drop bonuses (+30% / +35%).
- **Orb Forge facelift** — each card shows the color name above the icon and the ability's name below it, with the upgrade and its cost underneath.

## v1.9 (June 2026)

### Play Store "What's new" (short)

> New rank frames to earn and an animated Creator crest unlockable by code. Beat the campaign for a story ending and an exclusive MindMaster frame, plus your Multiplayer Specials unlock. Now on iPhone with Game Center sign-in, and the screen is locked to portrait. Pink & Teal orbs reworked into active deduction abilities. Fixes to daily points and the PvP Elo ladder.

### What's new in 1.9

- **More profile frames** — eight new illustrated, animated frames join the collection.
- **Unlock codes** — redeem a code in your Profile to unlock a frame. The first is the animated Creator crest (designed by the dev).
- **Campaign completion rewards** — defeat Mad Martin for a story ending (he names you a Battle-trained MindMaster), the exclusive **MindMaster** frame, and a reveal that Multiplayer Specials are now for sale in the Orb Forge.
- **iPhone support groundwork** — Game Center sign-in on iOS (Play Games on Android), so leaderboards and the ladder work across platforms.
- **Portrait lock** — the game now stays in portrait, the way it's designed to be played.
- **Pink & Teal rework** — both orbs now have a single ability in campaign and multiplayer: Pink "Reveal 2" (reveal 2 code positions) and Teal "Purge 3" (eliminate 3 colors not in the code).
- **⚔️ Gauntlet mode** — a new endless run from the title screen: face the MindBreaker through ever-harder codes, push your depth, bank fragments, and climb a global Gauntlet leaderboard.
- **🏅 Achievements** — 19 goals to chase across campaign, daily, gauntlet, and PvP, each with a fragment reward. View them from your Profile.
- **⭐ Boss mastery** — earn up to 3 stars per campaign boss (win, solve fast, or win with no orbs), shown on the boss cards.

### Fixes
- Daily Challenge now correctly records season points (fixed several server-side scoring bugs, including solves that used extra-guess orbs).
- PvP ranked duels now correctly update your Elo rating after a match.

## v1.8 (June 2026)

### Play Store "What's new" (short)

> Daily Challenge is here! Face the MindBreaker in a new puzzle every day — the same code for every player. Climb the new global Leaderboards and ranked PvP Ladder, build a daily Streak, and earn animated Profile Frames as you rise through the ranks. Plus one-tap ranked rematches, share your daily results, and a new rank tracker in Play Online. Lots of fixes too. Crack the code. Master the orbs.

---

### What's new in 1.8

**🗓️ Daily Challenge**
- A brand-new daily boss battle against the MindBreaker — one shared puzzle for every player, every day.
- Difficulty rotates daily (orb set size and pool), with bigger rewards on harder days and a guaranteed rare drop on Hard days.
- The match hands you free helper orbs on tougher days so anyone can take it on, no matter your altar rank or unlocked orbs.
- The MindBreaker arrives with his own boss art and battle themes that alternate day to day.
- Resets at your local midnight — same puzzle for everyone on the same date.

**🔥 Daily Streaks**
- Solve on consecutive days to build a streak (with a personal best).
- Longer streaks earn bonus season points — keep the fire going.

**🏆 Leaderboards & Ranking**
- New Leaderboard tab: see today's top solvers ranked by fewest turns and fastest time, with a Global / Friends filter.
- Your rank appears right on the results screen after you solve.
- Seasonal tiers: Novice → Cipher → Adept → Savant → MindBreaker → OrbMaster.

**⚔️ Ranked PvP Ladder**
- Play Online duels now feed a seasonal Elo skill rating.
- New "Your Rank" tracker in the Play Online lobby shows your tier and how far you are from the next rank.
- One-tap ranked **Rematch** to instantly run it back against the same opponent.

**🖼️ Profile Frames (new cosmetics)**
- Earn illustrated, animated avatar frames by reaching ranking tiers — permanently yours once unlocked.
- Equip them from your Profile; your frame shows on the home screen, your profile, and your leaderboard rows for rivals to see.
- Unlock by climbing **either** the daily season ranks or the PvP ladder.

**🏆 Campaign completion rewards**
- Beat Mad Martin to earn a story ending — he names you a Battle-trained MindMaster.
- Unlock the exclusive **MindMaster** profile frame, plus reveal modals announcing your new frame and that Multiplayer Specials are now for sale in the Orb Forge.

**🔮 Orb rebalance**
- Pink and Teal orbs now have a single ability in both campaign and multiplayer — Pink "Reveal 2" (reveal 2 code positions) and Teal "Purge 3" (eliminate 3 colors not in the code). Daily Challenge fragment income offsets the removed reward multipliers.

**📣 Share Your Results**
- After a daily solve, share a spoiler-free, emoji-grid result card with friends.

### Fixes
- Fixed friends showing as "offline" in Play Online even when both players were online.
- Fixed the Daily Challenge appearing to reset twice in one day (now resets at your local midnight).
- Fixed season points not being recorded for completed daily challenges.
