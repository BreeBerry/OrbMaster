Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play? Each boss hides a secret sequence of colored orbs. Make a guess, then read the feedback pegs — a red peg means right color in the right spot, a white peg means right color in the wrong spot — and use deduction to crack the code before you run out of turns.

What are Ability Orbs? Orbs you forge with fragments earned from battles. Equip up to four before a match to reveal positions, eliminate colors, gain extra turns, and more. You can also upgrade them in the Orb Forge.

Does my progress save? Yes — your campaign progress, orbs, and fragments are saved on your device.

Do I need an internet connection? Campaign and Gauntlet modes can be played offline. The Daily Challenge, online duels, and leaderboards require a connection.
