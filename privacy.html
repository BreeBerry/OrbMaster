Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

BreezyBee Games ("we," "our," or "us") develops and operates the mobile application OrbMaster (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you download, install, and play our Game.

1. Information We Collect

Offline / Single Player Mode: OrbMaster is built fundamentally to run client-side on your device. We do not require or collect personal information, user accounts, names, email addresses, or precise device locations to play the standard game modes.

Technical Data & Device Permissions: The Game may utilize underlying native framework tools via Capacitor (such as standard storage access) to store your unlockable orbs, item currency data, and campaign progress locally on your device. This data remains on your physical hardware and is not transmitted to us.

2. Online Multiplayer (Play Online Mode)

When you use the Play Online multiplayer feature, your device connects to Supabase — a third-party real-time infrastructure service — to facilitate live matches. During a multiplayer session, a randomly generated, non-personally-identifiable session ID and your in-game moves are broadcast through Supabase's servers. Supabase may process your IP address as part of establishing this connection. No personal information, account data, or device identifiers are intentionally transmitted. Multiplayer session data is ephemeral and is not stored in a persistent database by BreezyBee Games.

For more information on how Supabase handles data, please review the Supabase Privacy Policy.

3. Data Collection by Third Parties

We do not integrate third-party data tracking SDKs, ad networks, or user profiling analytical services within the application. No user data is harvested or shared with external corporate entities for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Children's Privacy

OrbMaster is a family-friendly strategic deduction challenge. Because we do not compile, store, request, or share any personal data from any user, our Game complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) standards.

5. Data Security

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that store your personal game data, and therefore we cannot access, modify, or delete it remotely.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to align with legal updates or new game features. We advise you to review this page periodically for any modifications. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect when the most recent changes were made.
